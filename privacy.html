Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What this policy covers

AdLens ("we," "our," "the app") helps Amazon sellers analyse their Sponsored Products advertising reports. This policy explains what information we collect, how it's used, and how it's protected.

Information we collect

We collect the following, and nothing more:

  • Account information: your email address, and if you sign in with Google, the basic profile information Google shares (name and email) as part of standard sign-in.
  • Uploaded report data: when you upload a Sponsored Products or Search Term report, its contents (campaign names, spend, sales, ASINs, search terms, and related advertising metrics) are processed to generate your analysis.
  • Your most recent analysis: we store only your single most recent processed report so you can return to it later. Uploading a new report replaces the previous one — we do not keep a history of past reports.
  • Feedback you submit: if you use the in-app feedback form, we store the message you write along with your account ID.

What we do not collect

  • We do not use advertising trackers, analytics pixels, or third-party marketing cookies.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.
  • We do not access your Amazon Seller or Advertising account directly — you choose what to upload, and only the file you upload is read.

How ASIN-to-title lookups work

To show readable product names instead of bare ASIN codes, AdLens looks up the public product title for each ASIN in your uploaded report directly from Amazon's public product pages. Only the ASIN code itself is sent for this lookup — no other data from your report is shared in this process. Resolved titles may be temporarily cached to avoid repeating identical lookups.

Where your data is stored

Your account and report data are stored using Supabase, a hosted database and authentication provider, with access restricted so that only you can read your own saved report. The app itself is hosted on Vercel. Google's sign-in service (Google OAuth) is used only to verify your identity when you choose to sign in with Google — we do not receive your Google password.

How long we keep your data

Your account and most recent saved report are kept for as long as your account exists. You can replace your saved report at any time by uploading a new one. If you'd like your account or data deleted entirely, contact us using the method below.

Your choices

  • You can log out at any time from the menu in the dashboard or the launchpad.
  • You can request deletion of your account and stored data at any time.
  • You can choose not to upload a report — the app simply won't have anything to analyse until you do.

Children's privacy

AdLens is intended for business use by Amazon sellers and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of AdLens after a change means you accept the updated policy.

Contact us

If you have questions about this policy or your data, or want it deleted, please use the Send feedback option in the app's menu (the hamburger icon on the dashboard) and let us know what you need — we'll respond directly.
